About Me

I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon, where I work on optimizing customers' payment experience through machine learning methods. My research interests span across generative models and biological materials for non-invasive devices.

I completed my PhD in Industrial Engineering at Purdue University in 2020, focusing on "Modeling and Variability Estimation of Network Collections". My work combines theoretical insights with practical applications in machine learning and network science.
